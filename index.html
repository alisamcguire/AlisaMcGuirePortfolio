Our Story

The Ulster Group Cafe is a Scott-Irish family owned business located in Huntsville, Alabama. The founder, Robert McGuire, is a retired Army Colenel who is passionate about his heritage, food, and family. After his retirement, Colenel McGuire visited the McGuire castle on a trip to the historic province of Ulster in Northern Ireland. There, he connected with traditional Irish culture and felt inspired to bring a taste of it back home with him. In 2024, him and his family opened the Ulster Group Cafe in Huntsville, Alabama to share some of this culture with his community.
